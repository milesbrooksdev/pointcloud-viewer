2020 — ORIGIN

The Spark

C4 was founded in a small studio in Lyon, born from a desire to bridge the gap between high-end fashion photography and generative digital art.

2022 — EXPANSION

Digital Fluidity

Our first major collaboration with global luxury houses allowed us to pioneer the "Organic Motion" framework, blending real-world physics with surrealist aesthetics.

2024 — TODAY

Future Casting

Relocating to Studio Blanc, we've expanded our capabilities into real-time interactive installations and neural-generated spatial environments.
